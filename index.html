11-3-15

Hikeing with Uncle Ben and Cooking The weather today could of turned out so cold today considering its November. What beautiful weather it was with an amazing 69 Degrees outside. It was so gorgeous outside today and made it such an amazing hike. My brother, I and our great friend Uncle Ben had such an amazing time hiking today. We were mainly just doing it for the cooking merit badge with Boy Scouts but also to see him. We decided to go to Prince William Forest park today as it's a great choice because it's close and has great wildlife and scenery to see.

The trail we were going to go on was 3.1 miles but we just did a big loop and instead we did 6.5 miles. We had breakfast at the picnic tables near the visitor center with bagels, bananas and grapes. The trees were so beautiful! After a few miles we stopped at the middle of the loop to have lunch. Spencer and I had some mountain house chicken and rice as uncle ben had trail tacos. I'll have to try them someday!! Also that's where we got to make some alcohol stoves and had some delicious food. Making the stoves was a great experience. With the alcohol stoves we just made, it took about fifteen minutes to boil a cup of water compared to using his real stove just taking two minutes to boil two cups of water. If you're stuck in a survival situation it's a great lightweight alternative but if I had the choice I wouldn't rely on it If I didn't need too. I loved stopping at the small little waterfall near the bottom of this huge hill where Uncle Ben got some water and filtered it. Spencer tried it too. Doing the 6.5 miles was perfect as it just meant more time to hang with Uncle Ben as we don't normally see each other a lot. He's such a cool and great guy to know. I also got to make, learn and use an alcohol stove which just adds to the experience. I'm looking forward to possibly doing it again just a different trail maybe we will go to Shenandoah national park next if we do plan another hike. Check out his Blog.
